DevOps training

Better, Stronger, Faster:- Master the DevOps model using open-source tools like Puppet, Chef, and cloud platforms such as AWS and Microsoft Azure.The Devops movement is bold enough to believe there is a better way of building teams and software that has rapidly spread across the development and operations community. The culture of a DevOps organisation and team is the heart of the movement and the most important pillar to implement. In this course you will explore the origins of the movement, the building blocks of Agile and LEAN and the cultural changes you and your organisation will need to adopt to become a successful DevOps team. In a blended mixture of theory, hands on, workshops and the use of tools such as GIT, SVN, JIRA you will learn how to plan, organise and work in an atmosphere of collective responsibility.


Objectives Of the Course

Understanding Cloud based tools and Cloud Models
Understanding LINUX basics and Administration
Working with Version Control and SVN
Working with GIT version control system
JENKINS and Continuous Integration
