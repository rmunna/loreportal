What Is Data Science

Data Science also known as data- driven science helps you to create models, methodologies, and algorithms that provide practical utility. Data science is also the practice of asking questions and finding solutions to unknown problems which in turn motivate business values. This process involves working with a set of existing data or defining the data all by you. The next step involves the collection, storage and exploration of data in huge volume which is often more than 1TB and has more than 1000 dimensions. This is followed by identifying the type of analysis to be done to get the answers. These analyses are performed by making use of various tools or algorithms seldom in a parallel or distributed architecture. Once the insights are gathered from the analysis, they are communicated in the form of dashboards or visualizations or simple stories so that it is easy for a non data scientist to understand and build a conversation using it.
